## About Me

Sr. Staff Engineer & Bar Raiser at Coupang, based in Seoul. Currently focused on AI — Agent architecture, LLM engineering, and how AI is reshaping the software industry. Prior 20 years of full-stack engineering background in Java, Android, Kotlin, and TypeScript.

My career path has been anything but typical — Samsung, DiDi, Kuaishou, Coupang — spanning China and Korea, from writing business logic to designing architecture to defining technical direction, and serving as a Bar Raiser to uphold hiring standards. At every turn, I chose not the highest-paying offer, but the one that would give me a different perspective.
